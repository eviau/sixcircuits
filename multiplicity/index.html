Multiplicity

In this one, we will create an encyclopedia together.

You may speak of things you saw in the forest - or imagine a whole new world. You can also talk about things you have seen in this world called Earth.

I will give you prompts, and you will give me descriptions.

The goal is to exhaust knowledge. Keep going.
